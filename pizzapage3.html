Humm Pizza!

Uptime recipie!

Directions time!

Now for the recipie!

  • 10 ounce pizza crust dough
  • 1 cup hummus spread
  • 1 1/2 cups sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup broccoli
  • 2 cups shredded montery jack cheese

Now for the steps!

  1. Step 1
    2.

    preheat oven to 475 degrees

  2. Step 2
    3.

    roll pizza crust and place on a pizza pan or backing sheet. spread thin layer of hummus over crust. Aranged sliced peppers and broccoli over hummus, and top it off wth shreeded cheese

  3. Step 3
    4.

    back in preheated oven until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted in the center for 10 to 15 minutes. Slice and serve it up!
