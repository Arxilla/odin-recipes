Pesos Pizza Recipie!

A different take on your regular pizza!

Follow the steps below to make a most delectible dish!

Ingredients!

  • 12 inch pre baked pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pesto
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 ounce can chopped black olives, drained
  • 1/2 small red onion, chopped
  • 4 ounce canned artichoke hearts, drained and sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Now for the ACTUAL steps!

  1. Step 1
    2.

    Preheat oven to 450 degreens F

  2. Step 2
    3.

    Spread pestos on pizza crust, top with tomatoes, bell peppers, olives, red onions, artichoke hearts and feta cheese

  3. Step 3

    4. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and browned
